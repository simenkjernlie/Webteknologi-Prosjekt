About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of young adults who have started a travel guide / travel agency for those who want to vacation in Sardinia. In the management we are four good friends, but we have many employees distributed over many offices, hotels and activity centers throughout Sardinia.

What do we do?

We are working to find the best deals on the best experiences, hotels and hospitality in Sardinia. We want to offer the very best for our customers and you will feel that you make the most of your holiday at a reasonable price. We travel a lot and test hotels and activities before we recommend them further to give you realistic recommendations and great deals.

What can we do for you?

We offer the best deals of all travel agencies in Sardinia. We have many employees ready to help you with what you may need. Our 24-hour phone makes us ready to assist you at any time. We offer activities for the whole family, couples or singles. We can also offer good group offers and activities. We hope you will be satisfied and will contact you if you have any questions.
